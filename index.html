Core Practice Areas

We offer bespoke, strategic legal advocacy across a wide spectrum of legal disciplines, maintaining exceptional success rates.

Civil Litigations & Property Disputes

Property claims, title verifications, inheritance, and real estate litigation.

Our civil and property division deals with high-stakes land dispute resolution, partition suits, easement rights, eviction, and landlord-tenant conflicts. We ensure rigorous verification of title deeds and represent clients before all tiers of civil courts.

  • Title Search & Property Due Diligence
  • Partition and Inheritance Suits
  • RERA & Real Estate Regulatory Appeals
  • Injunctions & Specific Performance Suits

Corporate & Business Law

Company incorporation, contract drafting, compliance, and corporate dispute resolution.

We act as legal counsel for startups, mid-sized firms, and corporate conglomerates, advising on corporate governance, mergers & acquisitions, venture capital deals, statutory compliances, and breach of contract litigation.

  • Master Service Agreements (MSA) & NDA Drafting
  • Shareholders Agreements & Equity Structuring
  • Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) Proceedings
  • Corporate Tax Advisories & Dispute Filing

Family & Divorce Disputes

Mutual consent divorce, child custody, alimony, and family asset distribution.

Providing compassionate, discrete, yet highly strategic advocacy for family disputes. We prioritize amicable settlements through mediation but offer formidable representation in court when required.

  • Divorce by Mutual Consent & Contested Divorce
  • Child Custody, Wardship & Guardianship
  • Maintenance, Alimony & Spouse Support
  • Domestic Violence Act & Restitution Rights

Criminal Defense & Bail Matters

Anticipatory bail, regular bail, white-collar crimes, and criminal trial advocacy.

Unwavering defense advocacy for individuals and corporations. We handle white-collar fraud, cybercrimes, quashing of FIRs, anticipatory bails, and trials with extreme confidentiality and strategic rigor.

  • Anticipatory & Regular Bail Petitions
  • White-Collar Crime & Financial Fraud Defense
  • FIR Quashing & Appeals before High Courts
  • Negotiable Instruments Act (Cheque Bounce Cases)
